Who We Are

Tirumala Dresses is a trusted uniform supplier based in Guntur, specializing in high-quality stitched uniforms for schools, hospitals, hotels, restaurants, and corporate organizations. With a strong focus on fabric quality, precision stitching, and customer satisfaction, we provide uniforms that represent professionalism, comfort, and long-term durability.

Our Journey

The journey of Tirumala Dresses began in 1960 as a small-scale tailoring effort, driven by passion and dedication. In the early days, the focus was on carefully stitching garments with attention to detail, and building trust with every customer. This strong foundation helped shape Tirumala Dresses into a reliable name in uniform stitching and institutional garment supply.

Our Journey

The journey of Tirumala Dresses began in 1960 as a small-scale tailoring effort, driven by passion and dedication. In the early days, the focus was on carefully stitching garments with attention to detail, and building trust with every customer.

Like any growing business, the path was not always easy. There were many challenges, struggles, and obstacles, but through hard work, persistence, and commitment to quality, Tirumala Dresses gradually expanded its reputation.

Over the years, the business evolved from a small local tailoring operation into a recognized uniform supplier, serving a wide range of institutions including schools, hospitals, hotels, restaurants, and corporate organizations.
